Mood Generator

Methodology of Visual Communication Course Created for NABA by Gilda Manfrin

Methodology

—

—

This tool randomly combines adjectives and nouns drawn from a cultural vocabulary developed through a qualitative mapping of art, design, and fashion publications supported by AI, to help students new to contemporary culture synthesize and update trends more effectively. Each word has an assigned color and sound; the generated background and sound are chords created by their sum.
