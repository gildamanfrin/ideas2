Content Strategy

Master in Fashion Marketing · IED, Milan
2024–2025 · 2025–2026

During this course, we will look at both consolidated practices and emerging approaches, treating content not as a secondary asset but as a primary tool to build solid identities and coherent brand meaning.

The course encourages an approach grounded in culture and values, rather than dependent on trends, algorithms, or platforms.

Alongside the work in class with the students, I have curated a series of talks through which students engage with seven professionals working at different levels and in different ways in content strategy, production, and analysis.

Throughout the series and each guest's intervention, we will explore the different stages and aspects of fashion visual assets and storytelling creation.

The goal is to give students the opportunity to understand how this is an ever-evolving and multifaceted area of work that can be seen and told from many different perspectives.

Considering the international background of the class, an exchange with either foreign professionals or Italian professionals working abroad enriches the experience.
