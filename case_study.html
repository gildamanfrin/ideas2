Case Study

Editorial Identity & Narrative Systems

2021–2023 · Strategic Consultancy & Creative Direction

Context

Corneliani is a menswear brand deeply rooted in sartorial tradition and Italian heritage. The project began with a central question: how can a brand built on product excellence develop an appealing cultural and editorial presence?

Rather than focusing on individual campaigns, the work explored how to reposition the brand through a broader narrative system—connecting craftsmanship, contemporary culture, and a renewed idea of masculine elegance.

The Strategic Challenge

From product-centric heritage to narrative authority

Luxury tailoring brands often communicate primarily through product imagery. The challenge was to build a framework capable of expanding the brand’s voice beyond garments, trying to gain a newer space for the brand.

The work developed as a series of projects rather than isolated campaigns, working on editorial series and multimedia initiatives able to build a broader narrative authority.

Project Ecosystem

The direction unfolded through a combination of editorial frameworks, cultural partnerships, visual narratives, product-focused mood content, and projects centered on craftsmanship.

What mattered most was not a single campaign, but the possibility of building a constellation of initiatives able to articulate the brand beyond product communication alone, spanning collaborations with innovative voices, visual and textual guides, behind-the-scenes atelier narratives, and a more editorial and colloquial tone while keeping the high visual standards of the brand.

Highlights

Selected Projects

A selection of initiatives developed within the broader narrative system, spanning editorial direction, cultural partnerships, sound, and product storytelling.

Independent Publishing Partnerships
Collaborations with independent magazines including Valet Magazine, which developed an editorial published both in print and across Corneliani’s social channels, and Sali & Tabacchi Journal, which created a reportage from Festivaletteratura Mantova, a major cultural event taking place in the brand’s hometown.
A Guide to Conscious Elegance
A multi-chapter editorial project exploring the culture of materials, garment care, and understated sartorial details through a more reflective and educational tone of voice.
Sound Playlists
A series of curated playlists conceived to extend the brand’s identity into a sonic dimension, connecting atmosphere, rhythm, and contemporary masculinity.
Atelier Project
A photographic series focused on the Corneliani atelier and the made-to-measure process, foregrounding gestures, tools, and forms of craftsmanship as part of the brand narrative.
Product Mood Series
A set of still life and detail-focused contents built around product mood, sartorial details, and tactile atmospheres, which achieved particularly strong resonance across social platforms.
