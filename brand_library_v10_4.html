Brand Library

A working reference · updated ongoing

Emerging and independent brands used
in fashion communication courses at NABA
and in consulting practice

An index of emerging brands collected over the last year through consulting practice and teaching at NABA and IED. The criteria: labels that depart, at least partially, from compulsive growth and visibility models — approaching the idea of a brand as something sustainable, experimental, or almost artistic, while remaining entirely wearable. Working with brands that hold values and follow through on them matters.
